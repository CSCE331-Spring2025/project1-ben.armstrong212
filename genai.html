Why Choose Texas A&M University?

Texas A&M University offers world-class education, a vibrant student life, and unmatched opportunities for growth. Here are some of the benefits of attending Texas A&M:

  • Top-ranked academic programs in engineering, business, agriculture, and more.
  • Strong alumni network known as the "Aggie Network," providing career support and mentorship.
  • A welcoming community that embraces diversity and inclusivity.
  • Affordable tuition and numerous scholarship opportunities.
  • State-of-the-art facilities and research opportunities for students and faculty.

Rich Traditions

Texas A&M University is steeped in traditions that make it truly unique. Here are a few:

  • Muster: A ceremony to honor Aggies who have passed away in the last year, emphasizing the Aggie Spirit.
  • 12th Man: Representing the dedication and loyalty of Aggie fans, who stand ready to support their team.
  • Reveille: The official mascot and the "First Lady of Aggieland," loved by all students and alumni.
  • Midnight Yell: A pep rally held the night before football games to boost morale and build camaraderie.
  • Silver Taps: A solemn ceremony held to honor students who have passed away.
